About Vazkeft LLC

Welcome to Vazkeft LLC, a cybersecurity service provider and vulnerability research company. Founded by Daniel Neustadter, Vazkeft is an entity for managing tax on bug bounty revenue and providing liability for penetration testing services. We are evolving into an experimental managed cybersecurity service provider and a hub for vulnerability research. Developing a pipeline for producing full stack web applications is a long-term goal.

At Vazkeft LLC, we continuously explore innovative approaches, including integrating CI/CD pipelines, to create and sell software solutions. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge cybersecurity expertise and empower businesses to navigate the complexities of today’s threat landscape.
