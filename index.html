About Vazkeft LLC

Welcome to Vazkeft LLC, a pioneering name in cybersecurity and security research. Founded by Daniel Neustadter, Vazkeft started as a platform to manage bug bounties and provide expert penetration testing services. Today, we are evolving into an experimental managed cybersecurity service provider and a hub for vulnerability research.

At Vazkeft, we continuously explore innovative approaches, including integrating CI/CD pipelines, to create and sell software solutions. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge cybersecurity expertise and empower businesses to navigate the complexities of today’s threat landscape.

Our Services

Vazkeft LLC offers a range of services tailored to meet the dynamic needs of our clients:

  • Managed Cybersecurity Services
  • Penetration Testing and Vulnerability Assessments
  • Bug Bounty Program Management
  • Security Research and Advisory
  • CI/CD Pipeline Development for Secure Software Delivery

Whether you need proactive security measures or advanced research insights, Vazkeft is here to help.
